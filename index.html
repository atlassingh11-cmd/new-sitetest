Meet Iffy

Advice built around your objective.

Iffy Khan specialises in Dubai and Abu Dhabi property, supporting buyers, sellers and investors with advisory-led guidance across off-plan, ready homes and the secondary market.

His approach starts with the client's objective, whether that is capital appreciation, rental yield, portfolio diversification or securing a long-term home. From area selection and market analysis to developer comparison, negotiation and due diligence, Iffy focuses on clarity, timing and long-term value.

The service is consultative, not pressured. Iffy's role is to explain the market clearly, challenge assumptions when needed and give honest advice, even when that means walking away from a deal.
